How points work

Win in 90' or extra time = +3 pts
Draw = +1 pt each
Penalty shootout win = +1 pt
Each goal scored = +1 pt
Clean sheet = +2 pts
Player total = sum of their 4 teams
What do these mean?

Played – games started. Counts live.

Result – 3 for win, 1 for draw, 0 for loss (penalty win = 1).

Goals – +1 per goal your team scores.

Clean Sheet – +2 if they concede 0 in that match.

Board page = total across all 4 teams. Click a name = breakdown per team.
